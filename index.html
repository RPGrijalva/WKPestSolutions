About Us

Here at WK Pest Solutions, we know how important it is to use products safe for pets and families because we have pets and families of our own. We're local. We're veteran-owned and operated. We're bonded and insured. We’re experienced in the world of pest solutions, and we’re here to help make sure your home or business is free from pests.
WK Pest Solutions offers both conventional and eco-friendly solutions to protect your home or business. We make quick and easy custom solutions whether you’re protecting your restaurant, commercial business site, classroom, kids room, home, office, or work space. You name it, we’re here to help, and look forward to new challenges.

Home

We know that protecting your high desert home is a safe haven for your kids, pets, and family is your number one priority. That's why it's ours too. We offer indoor and outdoor services for infestations and preventative care. Custom plans available.

Eco-Friendly

Our top requested eco product is ecovia, a thyme and rosemary based pest repellent that protects your home or business without the harsh chemicals found in conventional methods. We have glue traps available that are safer for the environment to catch and release pests without killing them. 

Commercial

We’re American Institute of Baking (AIB) Certified to work in commercial kitchens, and offer services such as drain cleaning, and COVID site disinfecting in addition to your pest prevention services. We also handle infestations so that you can get back to doing what you do best.
